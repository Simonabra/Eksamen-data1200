Student life

Student life can be a lot of fun, but it brings about some challenges when it comes to time management. Many new students are also new to the city and want to spend some time exploring and making new friends. Although important, a new student has many responsibilities of equal importance. Some students also work part-time jobs whilst studying. These students need some extra discipline in managing their schedule. As an IT student, you have several subjects that demand their share of your time and energy. Learning how to balance your efforts in each subject during the semester, is essential for your success as a student. Our group has done some reflections around this and about starting our IT studies at OsloMet.
